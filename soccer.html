Soccer

Soccer, also known as association football, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is played by 250 million players in over 200 countries and dependencies, making it the world's most popular sport. Soccer is a low-scoring game and is known for its passionate fans, intense rivalries, and global reach.

The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team. Goals can be scored by kicking the ball into the net, either directly from a shot or through a series of passes. The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.

Some of the most popular soccer leagues include the English Premier League, La Liga in Spain, Serie A in Italy, and the German Bundesliga. The FIFA World Cup, held every four years, is the most prestigious tournament in the sport and is watched by millions of fans worldwide.
