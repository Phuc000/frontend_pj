Quang Hai: A Questioning Return

Nguyen Quang Hai, who leave Ha Noi FC to play in Ligue 2 (France), has once said that he accept the challenge and hardness of playing football in France. However, he did not have a good performance there and is finding a way back to Ha Noi FC, where he again needs to face another problem.
